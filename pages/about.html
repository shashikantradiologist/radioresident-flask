About RadioResident

Empowering radiology residents with concise, high-yield learning tools.

Our Mission

RadioResident was created to bridge the gap between voluminous textbooks and the focused, exam-oriented content radiology residents actually need. We curate bite-sized notes, illustrative diagrams, mnemonics, and past-paper question banks so that you spend less time searching and more time mastering the material.

Who We Are

Founded in 2025 by Dr. Shashikant Morale, a practicing radiologist and passionate educator, RadioResident started as a set of handwritten “system-wise” guides for colleagues preparing for DNB and FRCR exams. Over time, those notes evolved into an online platform open to residents worldwide.

What You’ll Find Here

  • High-yield Notes » organ-wise and modality-wise summaries
  • Mnemonics & Flowcharts » visual memory aids
  • Past-Paper Question Bank » searchable, regularly updated
  • PDF downloads » printable handouts for quick revision

Roadmap

Over the next year we plan to:

  1. Launch interactive quizzes with instant feedback
  2. Add video case discussions and viva-voce simulations
  3. Introduce a peer mentorship program for new residents
  4. Integrate an AI-assisted reporting sandbox (powered by our RadioResident Report Lab)
